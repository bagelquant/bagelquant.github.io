This roadmap is organized around quantitative equity portfolio management. The core focus is on how alpha is generated, combined, and deployed into portfolios. Mathematical, econometric, and machine learning tools are treated as supporting foundations that enable the main investment framework.

## 0. Quick Start (High-Level Overview)

A fast, intuitive entry point to understand the end-to-end workflow of quantitative equity investing, without heavy mathematical detail:

- Introduction to quantitative equity management  
- Short version of mean-variance optimization  
- Short version of factor models  
- Short version of alpha research  

## 1. Core Financial Theory (Market & Pricing Foundations)

The core framework of equity investing and portfolio construction, involving substantial mathematical formulation and modeling:

- Modern portfolio theory  
- APT and factor models  
- Financial Reports  

## 2. Alpha Research (Signal Generation)

How predictive signals (alpha factors) are constructed, tested, and validated:

- Data  
- Alpha research  

## 3. Portfolio Construction (From Signals to Portfolios)

How individual alphas are combined and translated into investable portfolios:

- Linear combination  
- Non-linear alpha combination  
- Mean-variance optimization
