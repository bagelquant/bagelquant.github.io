The complete BagelQuant workflow is:

1. Build point-in-time [data](/research/data/).
2. Design and evaluate [factors](/research/factors/).
3. Combine signals into [predictions](/research/prediction/).
4. Translate forecasts into a [portfolio](/research/portfolio/).
5. Run a cost-aware [backtest](/research/backtest/).

Use [Learn](/learn/) when you need theory and [Research](/research/) when you want a practical sequence.
