The complete BagelQuant workflow is:

1. Build point-in-time [data](/zh/research/data/).
2. Design and evaluate [factors](/zh/research/factors/).
3. Combine signals into [predictions](/zh/research/prediction/).
4. Translate forecasts into a [portfolio](/zh/research/portfolio/).
5. Run a cost-aware [backtest](/zh/research/backtest/).

Use [学习](/zh/learn/) when you need theory and [研究](/zh/research/) when you want a practical sequence.
