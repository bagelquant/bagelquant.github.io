`bagelquant-bt` evaluates research outputs. It does not build signals or fetch
market data; it measures weight and factor DataFrames against price data.

## What It Contains

- Weight backtest engine.
- Factor evaluation with IC, quantiles, and top-N portfolios.
- Transaction-cost modeling.
- Structured result objects.
- Summary performance metrics.
- Visualization helpers.
